On the name

Why "Creative Dalaal"?

A dalaal is the one who makes the deal happen. Not the buyer, not the seller. The person in the room who gets it over the line. That's always been my role: turning creativity and tech into business results, and putting the right talent on the right project.

Over the years the brand grew, the team grew, and honestly, I grew a little apart from it. But "Creative Dalaal" stuck, because it stopped being an agency name and became shorthand for how I work. The legal entity behind it today is Alludere Media (OPC) Private Limited.

One thing to be straight about: the websites and development are mine, end to end. The brand, social and film work you'll see was delivered with my network, credited as the team effort it was.
