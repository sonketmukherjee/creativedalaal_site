The brief

The PR agency that needed its own PR.

Candid is one of East India’s top PR agencies — the brief was a site that matched that standing without over-engineering it.

It needed to prove credibility in seconds: who they’ve worked with, what people say, and an easy way to start a conversation.

The unique build

Portfolio, testimonials and lead capture on a single credible page.

I built a one-pager that leads with the work and the testimonials — social proof being the entire currency of PR — backed by a curated portfolio section.

Integrated contact forms turn that credibility into enquiries immediately, so an impressed visitor never has to go hunting for how to reach them.

The key moves.
