The brief

One page, every reason to visit.

Club Aeris didn’t need a sprawling website — it needed a single, confident page that captured the space, the menu and the specialities, and pushed people to act.

The other half of the brief was practical: delivery and booking platforms require a proper contact page for verification, so the site had to satisfy compliance while still feeling like a polished brochure.

The unique build

Swiggy & Zomato table-booking CTAs, woven into a one-page story.

The homepage walks a visitor through the highlights of the space, the menu and the restaurant’s specialities — then closes with direct Swiggy and Zomato CTAs for table booking and ordering, exactly where the intent peaks.

A dedicated contact page doubles as the compliance requirement for those platforms, so the integrations stay valid while also giving guests a clean way to reach the restaurant.

What I did

The key moves.

  1. 01

    Built a single-page narrative

    Space, menu, specialities and features in one confident scroll.

  2. 02

    Placed Swiggy & Zomato CTAs at the intent peak

    Booking and ordering right where the visitor is convinced.

  3. 03

    Added a compliance-ready contact page

    Satisfies platform verification and gives guests a direct line.

  4. 04

    Kept it fast and mobile-first

    Most restaurant traffic is on a phone, mid-decision.
