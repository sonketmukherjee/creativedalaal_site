The brief

Rebuild the store — while it keeps selling.

STRCH needed a full rebuild for conversion and brand, on a premium theme, designed properly in Figma first. The constraint: the existing store was live and trading, with apps already installed and configured.

So the real challenge was changing the engine while the car was moving — a clean rebuild with no downtime and no broken integrations.

The unique build

A sandboxed Figma-to-Shopify rebuild that went live intact.

I designed the store in Figma first, then built it on a premium theme in a sandboxed process — the new store assembled safely alongside the running one, with all pre-installed apps kept intact.

When it was ready, we went live with the new products and images in one clean switch — conversion-optimised layouts, brand-aligned banners, and no disruption to the business that was selling the whole time.
