The brief

A site that does the legwork.

IT Mind is an HR consultancy — their website needed to do two jobs at once: present their services credibly, and run a live job board that candidates could actually use to find and apply for roles.

Critically, it had to be admin-managed. New listings, applications and resumes all needed to flow without a developer in the loop — the team posts, candidates apply, vetting begins.

The unique build

An admin-posted job board with filter, sort, and resume-aware applications.

Admin uploads listings through the back-end; candidates filter and sort to surface the most relevant roles instead of scrolling a flat list. It turns a static careers page into a real, browsable job portal.

The application form gathers structured applicant details and accepts resume documents, then notifies admin the moment someone applies — so vetting starts immediately, with the CV already attached and ready to review.

What I did

The key moves.

  1. 01

    Built an admin-managed listings system

    The team posts and edits jobs themselves — no developer dependency.

  2. 02

    Added filter & sort for candidates

    Users zero in on the right roles fast, improving applications-per-visit.

  3. 03

    Created a resume-aware application form

    Captures applicant data and accepts CV uploads in one step.

  4. 04

    Wired instant admin notifications

    Every application alerts admin for immediate vetting — no missed candidates.

The outcome

Recruitment on autopilot.
